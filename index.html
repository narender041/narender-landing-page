FAQ

Questions brides ask before visiting

Can I bring reference designs from Instagram or Pinterest?

Yes. Bring screenshots, saved reels or reference photos. We will help you understand what can suit your event, comfort and budget.

Do you help with blouse design matching?

Yes. Blouse neckline, sleeve, back design and styling coordination are part of the consultation conversation.

How early should I start for bridal outfits?

For bridal and wedding functions, earlier is better. If your event is within 3–12 months, book a consultation now to avoid last-minute stress.

Can family members also get outfits?

Yes. Mothers, sisters, cousins and wedding family outfits can be discussed during consultation.
